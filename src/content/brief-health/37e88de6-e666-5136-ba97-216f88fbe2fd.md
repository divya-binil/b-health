This newest placement comes just weeks after Automation Anywhere was ranked No.1 in worldwide&nbsp;<a href="https://www.automationanywhere.com/company/press-room/automation-anywhere-ranked-1-for-public-cloud-rpa-market-share-by-leading-analyst-firm">public cloud RPA market share</a>&nbsp;for a second consecutive year by the global research firm IDC.&nbsp;</p><p>  “Companies are accelerating their adoption of automation and using the flexibility of our cloud platform to mitigate the impact of global events and navigate market challenges,” said Mike Micucci, Chief Operating Officer, Automation Anywhere. “As a pioneer in this space, we are proud to be recognized as a Leader in the Gartner Magic Quadrant for RPA for four consecutive years.
