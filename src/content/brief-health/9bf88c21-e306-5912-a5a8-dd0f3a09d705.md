Earlier this year, we reached out to healthcare providers and healthcare executives worldwide, and asked them a simple question: What do you believe will be the top healthcare challenges in the coming years?

The answers received were varied and covered many perspectives and concerns. However, there were three overarching themes that emerged. First, many healthcare leaders believe that healthcare is moving irrevocably towards a patient-centric business model, and systems need to adapt. Secondly, the demographic shift to an older patient population with chronic conditions needing care will have a massive impact on every aspect of health systems.
